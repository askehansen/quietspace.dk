The people behind

With a background in interior design from Parsons School of Design NYC and Paris, and an entrpreneural mind-set, Maria opened Tea Time, Copenhagen’s first modern tea parlour in 2003 and with that paved the way for the tea trend in Copenhagen. Tea Time was features in infinite national as well as international newspapers and magazines.

In 2019 Maria opened QuietSpace at Matrikel1 in the heart of Copenhagen, making it Copenhagen’s first in-house “mental fitness center”.

“QuiteSpace and Maria have given me the most important space when developing my business -the space for personal growth. Through its unique surroundings, most skilled meditation teachings & full hearted people, QuiteSpace provides an impactful space for reconnecting, restoring & re-imagination. All essential practices for having a mentally healthy and fulfilling life…”

- Cecilie Willer, Founder at Today

“QuietSpace has given the team of Impactly a new holistic perspective on what it means to work - and that working fast, rushing, stressing, achieving and so on, is not what it’s all about. It’s just as important to slow down, disconnect and allow yourself to come back from a different angle and just breath. It can be easy to gain tunnel vision when you are absorbed by something, that we need to allow ourselves the opportunity to pull back for a moment and simply enjoy the journey of life together…”

- Johan Dubert, Adm. Director at Impactly

“QuietSpace is a fantastisk space to re-charge mentally and find some peace of mind which enables me to make important work decisions..”
