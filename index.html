The people behind

With a background in interior design from Parsons School of Design NYC and Paris, and an entrepreneural mind-set, Maria opened Tea Time, Copenhagen’s first modern tea parlour in 2003 and with that paved the way for the tea trend in Copenhagen. Tea Time was featured in infinite national as well as international newspapers and magazines.

In 2019 Maria opened QuietSpace at Matrikel1 in the heart of Copenhagen, making it Copenhagen’s first in-house “mental fitness center”.
